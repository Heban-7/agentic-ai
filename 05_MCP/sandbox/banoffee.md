# Banoffee Pie Recipe

## Ingredients:
- 100g (3.5oz) digestive biscuits, crushed
- 75g (2.5oz) unsalted butter, melted
- 397g can of sweetened condensed milk
- 3 ripe bananas, sliced
- 300ml (10fl oz) double cream
- 1 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder (for dusting)
- 1 tablespoon dark chocolate shavings (optional)

## Instructions:
1. **Make the Base**: Mix the crushed biscuits with melted butter until fully combined. Press the mixture into the base of a 20-23cm (8-9 inch) springform cake tin. Chill in the fridge for at least 30 minutes.
2. **Prepare the Filling**: In a saucepan, gently cook the condensed milk over low heat, stirring constantly for about 15 minutes or until thick and golden. Pour it over the chilled biscuit base and spread evenly.
3. **Layer the Bananas**: Once the caramel is set, layer the banana slices over the top.
4. **Whip the Cream**: In a bowl, whip the double cream until it holds soft peaks. Spread over the bananas.
5. **Garnish**: Dust with cocoa powder and sprinkle with chocolate shavings, if desired.
6. **Serve**: Remove from the tin and serve chilled.
